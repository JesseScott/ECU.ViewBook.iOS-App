The Master of Digital Media (MDM) is Canada's first professional graduate program in digital media offered jointly by Emily Carr, University of British Columbia, Simon Fraser University, and the British Columbia Institute of Technology.  

Located at the Centre for Digital Media in Vancouver BC, this intensive program includes an internship and where you will engage in real world projects and coursework that provides valuable leadership experience, training, and top industry connections.  

When you graduate, you will be prepared for work at the highest level in the industry as creators, producers, innovators, or entrepreneurs.  

To find out more visit thecdm.ca.
