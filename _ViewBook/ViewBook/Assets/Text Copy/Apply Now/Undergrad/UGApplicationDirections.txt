STEP 1: 
Future undergraduate students review your academic eligibility. 

STEP 2: 
Apply online at https://applybc.ca (application opens September 15 and closes January 15).

STEP 3: 
Submit your high school and/or university/college transcripts (all transcripts are due January 15).

STEP 4: 
Prepare your visual art portfolio.

STEP 5: 
Submit your visual art portfolio online through SlideRoom (due February 1).

The University will contact you with a decision via regular mail posted no later than March 31.

For full admission details visit: ecuad.ca/admissions/undergrad
